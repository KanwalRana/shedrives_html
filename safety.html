    Drivers' safety tips

  • Before the ride
  • During the ride
  • After the ride

  • Fatigue behind the wheel is more dangerous than you think – please do not take orders if you feel tired or unwell.

  • Please double-check the final destination or pick-up point before accepting the order. If you feel unsafe to go somewhere, it’s better to skip this offer.

  • Check the passenger’s photo before starting a trip. Cancel the ride if the info doesn't match.

  • Changed your mind? Let the passenger know and cancel your trip.
